Series

Steal from software

Good industries steal. Surgeons took the checklist from aviation; hospitals took lean from Toyota. This series argues engineering design should take its document and data practices from software teams, who solved our coordination problems in the 1990s and left the tools lying around, free.

Six parts, published fortnightly.
